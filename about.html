A Life
Spent
Learning

An irritating jumble of broken links started my obsession with programming.

While working as a salesman for a wine importer, I realized we needed to modernize our website and fix all those broken links. As I began exploring web devleopement, our page views increased, my sales doubled, and I realized how much I enjoyed learning to code.

Soon, I found myself spending all my free time writing code.

Then Corona came.

Covid-19 has crippled the hospitality industry I love. But I took this catastrophe as an opportunity to launch a new career in web development.

Originally from California, I studied Spanish and Portguese at UC Berkeley. After school, I worked for years as an English teacher and translator in Madrid, Spain.

Later I moved to Stockholm, working for many years as a sommelier, server and, finally, in the wine import trade.

Constant learning has been the throughline of my life. In 8 different countries, I have worked with everything from sports reporting to farming fruit. I love meeting new people and especially trying new foods.

Aside from programming, I spend my free time playing with my two daughters and cooking extravagant meals with my wife.
